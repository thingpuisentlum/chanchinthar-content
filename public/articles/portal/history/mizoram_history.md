# Mizoram Chanchin: Hun Kal Tawh leh Hmasawnna

Mizoram, 'The Land of the Hill People' tia hriat hi India hmar thlang lama state pawimawh tak a ni a. He state-ah hian hmasawnna chi hrang hrang leh chanchin lungchhiat thlak leh hlimawm tak tak a thleng tawh thin a ni.

## 1. History (Chanchin)

Mizo hnam hi hmanlai atanga tlangsang hmun nuam tak thlang thin hnam kan ni a. British sorkar hnuaiha kan awm lai te leh UT kan nih hnu, a hnu leh-a State puitling kan nih thlengin kan kalphung hi a danglam nasa hle.

Kan pi leh pu te kha an lo huaisen em em a, an tlang thlan te pawh ral laka himna tur ngaihtuah chunga thlang thin an ni. Zawlbuk nun te kha Mizo tlangvalte tana tlawmngaihna leh huaisenna zirtirtu pawimawh tak a lo ni tawh thin.

## 2. Science & Technology

Tuna kan thupui pawimawh tak pakhat chu Mizoram-a science leh technology hmasawnna hi a ni. Internet-in Mizoram a rawn thlen atangin kan nunphung hi a danglam hlawk a. Tunah chuan khawvel thil thleng engkim hi kan hrethiam zung zung tawh a ni.

![Technology](https://picsum.photos/800/400?random=11)

Science zirna lamah pawh hma kan sawn chho zel a. Higher Secondary School hrang hrangah science stream thlang kan thahnem chho hle. Hei hian kan hmalam hun atan beiseina thar min pe a ni.

## 3. Environment (Kan Chhehvel)

Mizoram hian ramngaw mawi tak leh nungcha hrang hrang a nei a. Mahse, tunlai hmasawnna hian kan environment-ah nghawng tha lo a nei ve bawk thin.

![Environment](https://picsum.photos/800/400?random=12)

Tlangram kan nih avangin lo (Jhum cultivation) kan neih hian ram kan vát nasa thin a. Hei hian tuihna a tichhia a, boruak a tichhia bawk a ni. Kan environment hi kan venhim loh chuan kan hmalam hun hi a thim hle thei a ni.

## 4. Conclusion (Thutlipna)

A tawp nan chuan, Mizoram hi kan hmalam hun hmun nuam a nih zelna turin mahni mawhphurhna theuh hlen i tum ang u. Chanchin thar leh thu thar hrang hrangte hian min tiharh a, hma min sawn tir zel tur a ni.
