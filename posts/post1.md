# Khawvel huap chanchin thar pawimawh

He chanchin thar hi vawiin tlai dar 5:00-a thupuan chhuah a ni. 

### Thil Thleng Pawimawh
* UN Secretary General-in inremna a duh thu a sawi.
* Middle East lamah buaina a ziawm deuh tan.

Mizo tawng hian kan hrilhfiah ang a, hetiang hian a ni: 
"Khawvel pum huap hian inremna leh muanna a pawimawh hle a, ram hrang hrangte thawhhona hi a hlu a ni."

[Source: BBC Mizo]
